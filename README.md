## Overview

### "Is this home worth owning in 30 years?"

#### Link: (soon)

This app makes projected values of Florida homeowner's future home costs with an emphasis on the portion of increased insurance over the next 30 years. 

User's can input their basic home properity details and toggle a "what-if" insurance growth rate for varies possible rates:

* Graph visually shows the portion of mortage and tax cost with insurance cost on top for user's to intutively understand the "stacking" cost of owning the home in the future. 

* Table shows a simple cost breakdown along with color visual cues when the insurance is making up a significant portion of the homeowner's cost.
